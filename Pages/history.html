History

Ammoses Shellendorf and his leader Cap'n Cuttlefish led a team to fight against the Octarians and eventually won. Ammoses's grandson Sheldon took a great interest in his military stories and was fascinated in weapons and later created Ammo Knights to support the Inklings in the fight against the Octarians.
